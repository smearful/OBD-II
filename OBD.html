Our Mission

Our mission at AutoScan is to revolutionize the automotive industry by providing a cutting-edge On-Board Diagnostics system that enhances the driving experience for our customers. Our system enables drivers to monitor the performance of their vehicles, track fuel efficiency, diagnose issues and increase safety on the road.

At AutoScan, we believe that a well-maintained vehicle is not only cost-efficient but also contributes to a cleaner environment. With our OBD-II system, we aim to empower drivers to take control of their vehicles' health and performance, by providing them with real-time data and insights that were once exclusive to professional mechanics.

Our OBD-II system is designed with user-friendliness in mind, making it accessible to all drivers, regardless of their technical background. We strive to educate our customers on the importance of vehicle maintenance and safety, and provide them with the necessary tools to achieve this.

We are committed to advancing the OBD technology by continuously researching and developing new features that address the evolving needs of our customers. Our team of experts is dedicated to delivering a system that exceeds our customers' expectations and provides unparalleled support throughout their driving journey.

Your Device

Common Error Codes:

P0420 Code: Catalyst System Efficiency Below Threshold (Bank 1)

U0100 Code: Lost Communication with ECM/PCM "A"

P0300 Code: Random or Multiple Cylinder Misfire Detected

P0340 Code: Camshaft Position Sensor "A" Circuit Malfunction

P0446 Code: Evaporative Emission Control System Vent Control Circuit Malfunction

P0430 Code: Catalyst System Efficiency Below Threshold (Bank 2)

P0011 Code: "A" Camshaft Timing Over Advanced (Bank 1)

P2096 Code: Post Catalyst Fuel Trim System Too Lean (Bank 1)

P0113 Code: Intake Air Temperature Sensor 1 Circuit High Input
